MISSION PARAMETERS

The Thermal Barrier

Spacecraft entering atmospheres like Mars or Earth face the "Entry Interface"—a wall of superheated plasma reaching 3,000°C. Current ablative shields are heavy, static, and single-use.

The Adaptive Solution

The GUIN Shield utilizes morphing geometry. By deploying inflatable sections reinforced with silica aerogels, we dynamically alter the drag coefficient ($C_d$) to ensure payload survival.
