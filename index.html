INNOVATION OVERVIEW

The Concept

Inspired by modern space engineering, our innovation prioritizes durability and flexibility. Unlike standard single-use shields, the GUIN Shield offers a longer career lifespan and is capable of handling multi-variable landing conditions without sustaining critical damage.

Performance Objectives

The system is designed to fit the rocket's specific mass and volume. By deploying motor-arms upon atmospheric entry, we achieve a direct deceleration of 15-20%, proportionally reducing heat intake and protecting the spacecraft throughout the descent.

02

SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

GEO DOUBLE-CONE GEOMETRY

Modified using CAD technologies, the shield features a double-cone shape with motor-arms and flaps for precise deployment and retraction.

MAT THERMAL ABLATION

Utilizing super-light thermal-protect ablation material. It locks heat away with minimal space occupancy, optimizing the mass-to-protection ratio.

OPS RETRACTION SEQUENCE

Post-landing, the motor-arms detract the shield to protect the mechanism for future use, validated via CFD analysis for heat and pressure resilience.
